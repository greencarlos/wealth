© DANIEL FRIED

WILLIAM GREEN has written for Time, Fortune, Forbes, The New Yorker, The Economist, and numerous other publications. As an editor and coauthor, he has collaborated on several books, including Guy Spier’s highly praised memoir, The Education of a Value Investor. Born and raised in London, Green studied English literature at the University of Oxford and received a master’s degree in journalism at Columbia University. He lives in New York with his wife and their two children.
